## The Cycle

### 1. Encounter
You find something in Garden or Wild that resonates.

A portal, a question, an exploration, a tangle.

**Bring it here.**

### 2. Explore
Sit with it in your Wild Garden.

Let order and chaos coexist.

Don't try to resolve it.

Just be with it.

### 3. Integrate
Notice what emerges.

Log breakthroughs and reflections.

Let insights ripen.

### 4. Prepare
When something feels ready to share:

Move it to `/ready_to_share`

Decide: Garden (structured) or Wild (raw)?

### 5. Contribute
Create a PR to the appropriate repo.

Share what emerged.

Let it flow back into the system.

### 6. Continue
The cycle never ends.

New encounters lead to new explorations.

New integrations lead to new contributions.

**This is the living process.**
